Who We Are

We are a small team of adventurous foodies who are always looking for something new to cook up! Initially, we started sharing recipes by trading cook books and paper scraps. But, when those were lost or covered in spilled milk, the idea for Scratch was born. Over the past 5 years we have sourced hundreds of thousands of your favorite recipes and added them to our database so you can access them anytime. I hope you enjoy! Bon appétit!
